Configurer Boutique en ligne

This guide will help you configure your online shop settings in Estoriom.

Accédez à demo-pos.estoriom.com

1. Cliquer sur "Online Shop"

Click the Online Shop menu to access your boutique's main settings and begin customization.

2. Entrer le nom de votre boutique en ligne

Click the Shop title field to prepare for editing your boutique's name and branding.

3. Importer le logo

Click the Shop logo field to update or change the visual branding of your online boutique.

4. Cliquer sur le lien pour se diriger vers la boutique

Click the shop link to access your online store settings and manage your shop details within Estoriom.

5. Mettre à jour la boutique

Click the Update button to save all your changes and apply the new settings to your online shop.
You have successfully configured your online shop settings in Estoriom, including updating the shop title, logo, and other options to personalize your boutique.
